what is ugadi all about ?

well ugadi is basically a new year festival for south indians from where you can start carving on some bright yellow mangoes 😋 i personally love this festival we also get to eat a soup (ugadi pachadi) in which there are many herbs and fruits all mixed togheter to form a nice sour and sweet taste which is good for health
